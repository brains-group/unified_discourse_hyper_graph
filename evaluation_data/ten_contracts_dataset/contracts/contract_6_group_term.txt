SECTION 1 - ABOUT THIS CERTIFICATE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1.1 This certificate is evidence of coverage under the Master 
    Group Policy issued to your employer.

1.2 The Master Policy controls in case of any conflict with this 
    certificate.

1.3 Your coverage is subject to the terms, conditions, and 
    exclusions in the Master Policy and this certificate.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2 - ELIGIBILITY AND EFFECTIVE DATE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2.1 You became eligible for coverage on your date of hire.

2.2 Basic coverage became effective automatically without 
    requiring evidence of insurability.

2.3 Voluntary coverage required enrollment within 31 days of hire 
    (Late Entrant provisions apply if enrolling after 31 days).

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3 - BENEFIT AMOUNT CALCULATION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.1 BASIC LIFE: Your basic benefit equals 2 times your annual 
    base salary, rounded to the next higher $1,000.

3.2 VOLUNTARY SUPPLEMENTAL: You may elect additional coverage in 
    increments of $10,000, up to a maximum of 5 times salary.

3.3 SALARY CHANGES: Your basic benefit will be recalculated 
    annually on your policy anniversary based on your current 
    salary.

3.4 MAXIMUM BENEFIT: Total coverage cannot exceed $1,000,000.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 4 - WHEN COVERAGE ENDS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
4.1 Your coverage terminates on the earliest of:
    (a) The date the Master Policy terminates
    (b) The last day of the month in which you cease to be an 
        eligible employee
    (c) The date you cease active full-time employment
    (d) The date you request termination of voluntary coverage
    (e) The last day for which required premiums have been paid

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 5 - CONVERSION PRIVILEGE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5.1 If your group coverage terminates for any reason except 
    non-payment of premium, you may convert to an individual 
    whole life policy without evidence of insurability.

5.2 You must apply for conversion within 31 days of termination.

5.3 The conversion policy will be issued at:
    • Your attained age at conversion
    • Standard premium rates for the individual policy
    • Maximum amount: Lesser of group coverage or $200,000

5.4 No medical exam or health questions required for conversion.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 6 - PORTABILITY
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6.1 If you leave employment, you may port your coverage by 
    continuing it as an individual policy.

6.2 Ported coverage continues at the same amount you had under 
    the group policy.

6.3 You must apply within 31 days of termination of employment.

6.4 Premium rates will be age-banded group rates, payable directly 
    to the insurance company.

6.5 Evidence of insurability is NOT required for portability.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 7 - ACCELERATED DEATH BENEFIT
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
7.1 If you are diagnosed with a terminal illness with life 
    expectancy of 12 months or less, you may request acceleration 
    of up to 75% of your death benefit.

7.2 Minimum acceleration: $5,000
    Maximum acceleration: $250,000

7.3 Accelerated amount will be reduced by an actuarial discount.

7.4 Any amount accelerated reduces the death benefit payable to 
    beneficiaries.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 8 - EXCLUSIONS AND LIMITATIONS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
8.1 SUICIDE: If death occurs by suicide within 12 months of the 
    coverage effective date, we will not pay a death benefit.

8.2 PRE-EXISTING CONDITION LIMITATION: None. Group coverage has 
    no pre-existing condition exclusions.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 9 - BENEFICIARY DESIGNATION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
9.1 You may change your beneficiary at any time by submitting a 
    change form to your employer's HR department.

9.2 If no beneficiary survives you, benefits will be paid to 
    your estate.

9.3 If you name multiple beneficiaries, benefits are divided 
    equally unless you specify percentages.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 10 - FILING A CLAIM
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
10.1 Beneficiaries should notify Alliance Group Benefits 
     immediately upon death of the insured employee.

10.2 Required documents:
     • Completed claim form (available from employer or insurer)
     • Certified death certificate
     • Proof of beneficiary identity

10.3 Submit claims to:
     Alliance Group Benefits - Claims Department
     1200 Insurance Plaza, Dallas, TX 75201
     Claims Hotline: 1-888-555-CLAIM

10.4 Benefits will be paid within 10 business days of receipt of 
     complete claim documentation.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 11 - WAIVER OF PREMIUM
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
11.1 If you become totally disabled before age 60 and remain 
     disabled for 6 consecutive months, your voluntary life 
     insurance premiums will be waived.

11.2 Coverage continues without premium payment during disability.

11.3 You must provide proof of continuing disability annually.

11.4 Waiver ends when you return to work, recover, or reach age 65.
