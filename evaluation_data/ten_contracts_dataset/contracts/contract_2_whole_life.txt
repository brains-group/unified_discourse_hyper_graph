4. KEY DEFINITIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"Cash Surrender Value" - The amount available if the policy is 
surrendered, equal to the cash value minus any outstanding loans 
and surrender charges.

"Dividend" - A return of premium that may be declared annually 
by the Board of Directors based on company experience.

"Paid-Up Insurance" - A reduced death benefit with no further 
premiums required.

"Policy Loan" - A loan made by us using the cash value as collateral.

    Guaranteed cash value at end of Year 20: $71,200

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
7. POLICY LOANS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
7.1 You may borrow against the cash value after the policy has 
    been in force for one full year.

7.2 Maximum loan amount: 90% of current cash surrender value.

7.3 Loan interest rate: 5% per annum, compounded annually.

7.4 Interest may be paid annually or added to the loan balance.

7.5 If total indebtedness exceeds the cash value, the policy 
    will terminate unless sufficient payment is made within 
    31 days of notice.

7.6 Outstanding loans and accrued interest will be deducted 
    from the death benefit or cash surrender value.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
8. DEATH BENEFIT PAYMENT
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
8.1 Upon receipt of proof of death, we will pay the beneficiary:
    Face Amount + Paid-Up Additions - Policy Loans - Loan Interest

8.2 Payment will be made within 7 days of receiving complete 
    claim documentation.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
9. NON-FORFEITURE OPTIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
If you stop paying premiums after the policy has been in force for 
three years, you may elect:

9.1 CASH SURRENDER: Receive the cash surrender value and 
    terminate the policy.

9.2 REDUCED PAID-UP INSURANCE: Continue coverage for a reduced 
    death benefit with no further premiums required. Amount 
    based on cash value and insured's attained age.

9.3 EXTENDED TERM INSURANCE: Continue the full face amount for 
    a limited period based on cash value. No further premiums.

9.4 If no election is made within 60 days of lapse, Extended 
    Term Insurance will automatically apply.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
10. REINSTATEMENT
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
10.1 You may reinstate this policy within 3 years of lapse if:
     (a) You provide evidence of insurability satisfactory to us
     (b) You pay all overdue premiums with interest at 6% per annum
     (c) You repay or reinstate any policy loans with interest

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
11. EXCLUSIONS AND LIMITATIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
11.1 SUICIDE: If the insured dies by suicide within two years 
     from the issue date, we will pay only the premiums paid, 
     without interest.

11.2 MISSTATEMENT OF AGE: If the insured's age was misstated, 
     we will adjust the face amount to what the premium would 
     have purchased at the correct age.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
12. FREE LOOK PERIOD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
You may cancel this policy within 30 days of receipt for a full 
refund of premiums paid.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
13. CONTESTABILITY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
We may contest this policy only within the first two years while 
the insured is living, and only for material misrepresentation in 
the application.
