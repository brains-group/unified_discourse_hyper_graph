SECTION 1: POLICY FEATURES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
1.1 This is a Return of Premium (ROP) Term Life Insurance Policy.

1.2 UNIQUE FEATURE: If the insured survives the 20-year term and 
    all premiums have been paid, we will return 100% of all 
    premiums paid ($32,400) to the policy owner.

1.3 This policy combines:
    • Life insurance protection during the term
    • Return of all premiums if you outlive the term
    • Option to convert to permanent insurance

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 2: DEATH BENEFIT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
2.1 If the insured dies during the policy term while the policy 
    is in force, we will pay $300,000 to the beneficiary.

2.2 The death benefit is payable in addition to any proportional 
    premium refund (see Section 3).

2.3 Beneficiaries must submit:
    • Certified death certificate
    • Completed claim form
    • Government-issued ID

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 3: RETURN OF PREMIUM PROVISIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
3.1 END OF TERM ROP:
    If the insured is alive on September 15, 2044, and all 
    premiums have been paid, we will pay the policy owner 
    $32,400 (100% of premiums paid over 20 years).

3.2 EARLY SURRENDER ROP:
    If you surrender this policy before the end of term:
    
    Years 1-5:   0% premium return
    Years 6-10:  30% of premiums paid returned
    Years 11-15: 60% of premiums paid returned
    Years 16-19: 90% of premiums paid returned
    Year 20:     100% of premiums paid returned

3.3 DEATH DURING TERM:
    If death occurs during the term, beneficiaries receive:
    • Full death benefit ($300,000), PLUS
    • A proportional premium refund based on the early surrender 
      schedule above

    Example: If death occurs in Year 8, beneficiaries receive:
    $300,000 death benefit + (30% × $12,960 premiums paid) = 
    $300,000 + $3,888 = $303,888

3.4 ROP payments are made within 60 days of the applicable date.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 4: PREMIUM PAYMENT REQUIREMENTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
4.1 Annual premium of $1,620 is due each September 15.

4.2 Premiums are LEVEL and will not increase during the term.

4.3 CRITICAL: To receive the full return of premium at end of 
    term, ALL premiums must be paid on time.

4.4 Grace period: 31 days. If premium is not paid within grace 
    period, policy lapses and return of premium benefit is 
    forfeited.

4.5 Missed premiums cannot be made up. If a premium is paid late 
    (during grace period), the ROP benefit is reduced by 10% for 
    each late payment.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 5: CONVERSION PRIVILEGE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
5.1 You may convert this policy to a permanent life insurance 
    policy without evidence of insurability.

5.2 Conversion available:
    • At any time before the policy expires, OR
    • Before the insured's 65th birthday, whichever is earlier

5.3 Minimum conversion amount: $50,000
    Maximum conversion amount: $300,000 (current face amount)

5.4 Upon conversion:
    • New policy premium based on attained age
    • ROP benefit is forfeited
    • Premiums paid to date are NOT returned

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 6: POLICY LOANS AND WITHDRAWALS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
6.1 Policy loans are NOT available under this policy type.

6.2 Partial withdrawals are NOT permitted.

6.3 The only way to access cash value is through:
    • Full surrender (with applicable ROP percentage), OR
    • Waiting until end of term for full ROP payment

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 7: EXCLUSIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
7.1 SUICIDE: If the insured dies by suicide within 24 months of 
    the issue date, we will pay only the premiums paid without 
    interest. No death benefit is payable.

7.2 MATERIAL MISREPRESENTATION: We may contest the policy within 
    2 years of issue if there was material misrepresentation in 
    the application.

7.3 NO OTHER EXCLUSIONS: No war, aviation, travel, or hazardous 
    activity exclusions apply.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 8: REINSTATEMENT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
8.1 If the policy lapses, you may apply for reinstatement within 
    5 years if:
    • You provide satisfactory evidence of insurability
    • You pay all past due premiums with interest at 6% per annum
    • The insured is still alive

8.2 Upon reinstatement, the ROP benefit is recalculated to exclude 
    the lapsed period.

8.3 A new 2-year suicide and contestability period begins upon 
    reinstatement.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 9: IMPORTANT POLICY NOTES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
9.1 This policy costs more than standard term life insurance 
    because of the return of premium feature.

9.2 The ROP benefit is NOT considered a taxable gain when paid 
    (consult your tax advisor).

9.3 This policy does NOT build cash value that can be borrowed 
    against during the term.

9.4 MISSTATEMENT OF AGE: If age was misstated, we will adjust 
    the death benefit to what the premium would have purchased 
    at the correct age. ROP amount is not affected.
