ARTICLE I - NATURE OF COVERAGE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1.1 This is a joint life insurance policy insuring two lives.

1.2 DEATH BENEFIT TRIGGER: The death benefit is payable only upon 
    the death of the SECOND insured to die (survivorship basis).

1.3 If the first insured dies, no death benefit is paid and the 
    policy continues in force covering the surviving insured.

1.4 The death benefit is paid only after both insureds have died.

1.5 PURPOSE: This policy is designed for estate planning and 
    estate tax liquidity purposes.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE II - DEATH BENEFIT PROVISIONS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2.1 Upon the death of the second insured, we will pay $2,500,000 
    to the designated beneficiary, minus any outstanding loans.

2.2 Proof of both deaths must be submitted:
    • Death certificate of first insured
    • Death certificate of second insured
    • Completed claim forms

2.3 Payment will be made within 30 days of receiving complete 
    documentation.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE III - FIRST DEATH BENEFITS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.1 When the first insured dies, no death benefit is paid, but:
    (a) Policy continues in force
    (b) Premium payment obligation continues
    (c) Policy owner receives written confirmation
    (d) Death certificate must be filed with us

3.2 The surviving insured becomes a single-life whole life policy 
    for administrative purposes only. Coverage amount and premium 
    remain unchanged.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE IV - PREMIUM STRUCTURE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
4.1 PREMIUM GUARANTEE PERIOD: Annual premium of $32,400 is 
    guaranteed level for the first 10 policy years.

4.2 AFTER YEAR 10: Premium may increase but will never exceed 
    the maximum guaranteed premium of $48,600 per year.

4.3 Premiums are payable annually on August 20th.

4.4 FIRST DEATH PREMIUM CONTINUATION: Premiums continue after 
    the first death at the same rate.

4.5 WAIVER OPTION: Premium waiver is not available on joint 
    survivorship policies.

4.6 GRACE PERIOD: If payment is not received, you have 60 days 
    to pay before the policy cancels.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE V - CASH VALUE AND LOANS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5.1 This policy accumulates cash value on a guaranteed schedule.

5.2 Projected cash value (based on current assumptions):
    End of Year 10: $285,000
    End of Year 20: $712,000

5.3 POLICY LOANS:
    • Available after policy has been in force for 2 years
    • Maximum: 90% of cash surrender value
    • Interest rate: Variable, currently 5.5% per annum
    • Loans reduce death benefit dollar-for-dollar
    • Available before or after first death

5.4 WITHDRAWALS: Partial withdrawals are not permitted under 
    this policy type.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE VI - SPLIT OPTION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6.1 Upon the first death, the policy owner may elect to split 
    this policy into two separate single-life policies, one on 
    each insured.

6.2 Requirements for split:
    • Must elect within 2 years of first death
    • Each policy's face amount determined by actuarial allocation
    • Both insureds must be alive at time of first death 
      (available only in rare simultaneous death situations with 
      medical uncertainty)
    • Evidence of insurability may be required

6.3 This option is rarely exercised and primarily exists for 
    divorce or trust modification situations.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE VII - EXCLUSIONS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
7.1 SUICIDE CLAUSE: If either insured commits suicide within 
    2 years of the issue date, the death benefit is limited to 
    return of premiums paid.

7.2 This suicide clause resets if the policy is reinstated.

7.3 CONTESTABILITY: We may contest this policy for material 
    misrepresentation within 2 years of issue while both insureds 
    are living.

7.4 NO OTHER EXCLUSIONS: No war, aviation, or travel exclusions 
    apply to this policy.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE VIII - ESTATE TAX PLANNING PROVISIONS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
8.1 This policy is owned by an irrevocable trust to exclude 
    proceeds from the insureds' taxable estates.

8.2 IMPORTANT TAX NOTE: Proceeds paid to the trust should not be 
    includable in either insured's estate if the trust is properly 
    structured and maintained.

8.3 Consult with estate planning attorney regarding:
    • Three-year lookback rule if policy transferred to trust
    • Crummey power requirements
    • Annual exclusion gift planning

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE IX - SIMULTANEOUS DEATH
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
9.1 If both insureds die in the same accident or incident and it 
    cannot be determined who died first, the death benefit will 
    be paid immediately.

9.2 If deaths occur within 30 days of each other, this will be 
    treated as simultaneous for claim purposes.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE X - OWNERSHIP AND ASSIGNMENT
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
10.1 The policy owner (the Trust) has all ownership rights 
     including the right to:
     • Designate and change beneficiaries
     • Assign the policy
     • Take policy loans
     • Surrender the policy

10.2 Neither insured has ownership rights unless specified in 
     the trust document.

10.3 Policy may not be assigned without trustee authorization.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE XI - FREE LOOK PERIOD
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The policy owner may cancel within 30 days of policy receipt for 
a full refund of premium paid.
