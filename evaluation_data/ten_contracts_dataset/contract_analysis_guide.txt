CONTRACT SUMMARY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. BASIC TERM LIFE (SecureLife Insurance)
   - Type: 20-year level term
   - Key Features: Simple structure, conversion privilege
   - Focus: Foundational concepts, basic beneficiary structure
   - Unique: Minimal exclusions (suicide only)

2. WHOLE LIFE WITH DIVIDENDS (Guardian Family Protection)
   - Type: Participating whole life
   - Key Features: Cash value, dividends, policy loans, 
                   non-forfeiture options
   - Focus: Permanent insurance, investment component
   - Unique: 4 dividend options, detailed loan provisions

3. UNIVERSAL LIFE (FlexProtect Insurance)
   - Type: Flexible premium universal life
   - Key Features: Flexible premiums, cost of insurance charges,
                   interest crediting, withdrawals
   - Focus: Unbundled product structure, premium flexibility
   - Unique: Detailed deduction mechanics, surrender charges

4. VARIABLE UNIVERSAL LIFE (Pinnacle Investment Life)
   - Type: Variable UL with sub-accounts
   - Key Features: Investment options, unit values, M&E charges,
                   fund expenses, securities regulation
   - Focus: Investment risk, complex fee structure
   - Unique: War and aviation exclusions, SEC disclosure

5. FINAL EXPENSE (ComfortCare Senior)
   - Type: Simplified issue whole life
   - Key Features: Graded death benefit, guaranteed issue,
                   no medical exam, small face amount
   - Focus: Senior market, underwriting alternatives
   - Unique: Graded benefit schedule, accidental death exception

6. GROUP TERM LIFE (Alliance Group Benefits)
   - Type: Employee group coverage
   - Key Features: Employer-sponsored, conversion rights,
                   portability, salary-based benefit
   - Focus: Group insurance concepts, employment relationship
   - Unique: Waiver of premium for disability, late entrant rules

7. TERM LIFE WITH RIDERS (ProtectPlus Family)
   - Type: 30-year term with 6 riders
   - Key Features: Comprehensive rider package, waiver of premium,
                   accelerated benefits, children's coverage
   - Focus: Rider mechanics, living benefits
   - Unique: Aviation waiver, guaranteed insurability option

8. JOINT SURVIVORSHIP (Estate Planning Life)
   - Type: Second-to-die whole life
   - Key Features: Two insureds, estate planning focus, trust ownership,
                   first death provisions
   - Focus: Joint life concepts, estate tax planning
   - Unique: Split option, simultaneous death clause

9. RETURN OF PREMIUM TERM (ValueGuard Life)
   - Type: 20-year ROP term
   - Key Features: Premium refund at maturity, graded surrender values,
                   no loans/withdrawals
   - Focus: Hybrid term structure, maturity benefits
   - Unique: Proportional death benefit, ROP schedule

10. INDEXED UNIVERSAL LIFE (Horizon Wealth Protection)
    - Type: Equity-indexed UL
    - Key Features: Index accounts, cap/floor/participation rates,
                    crediting methods, market protection
    - Focus: Alternative crediting, market-linked products
    - Unique: Point-to-point method, multiple indices
