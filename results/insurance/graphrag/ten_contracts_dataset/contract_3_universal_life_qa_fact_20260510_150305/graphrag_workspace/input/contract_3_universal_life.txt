ARTICLE 1 - DEFINITIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
1.1 "Account Value" means the accumulation of premiums paid, less 
    deductions for monthly charges and expenses, plus interest 
    credited.

1.2 "Cost of Insurance" (COI) means the monthly charge for the 
    death benefit based on the insured's age, sex, and risk class.

1.3 "Monthly Deduction Date" is the 10th day of each month.

1.4 "Surrender Charge" is a percentage-based charge applied if the 
    policy is surrendered in the first 15 years.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 2 - FLEXIBLE PREMIUM PAYMENTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
2.1 You may pay premiums at any time and in any amount, subject 
    to the following limits:

    Minimum: $75 per payment
    Maximum: $50,000 per year (subject to tax limitations)

2.2 Premiums are allocated to the Account Value after deduction 
    of a 3% premium expense charge.

2.3 You may skip payments as long as the Account Value is 
    sufficient to cover monthly deductions.

2.4 If Account Value falls below the amount needed to pay monthly 
    charges, the policy will enter a 61-day grace period.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 3 - MONTHLY DEDUCTIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
3.1 On each Monthly Deduction Date, we deduct from Account Value:
    (a) Cost of Insurance charge
    (b) Administrative expense charge: $8.00/month
    (c) Any outstanding policy loan interest

3.2 Cost of Insurance is calculated as:
    (Specified Amount - Account Value) × Monthly COI Rate / 1,000

3.3 COI rates are guaranteed not to exceed those shown in the 
    rate schedule attached to this policy.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 4 - INTEREST CREDITING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
4.1 Interest is credited to Account Value daily based on the 
    current credited interest rate.

4.2 Guaranteed minimum rate: 2.0% per annum
    Current rate: 3.5% per annum (subject to change monthly)

4.3 We may declare different rates for different policy years or 
    account value amounts.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 5 - DEATH BENEFIT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
5.1 OPTION A (Level Death Benefit) - Currently Selected
    Death Benefit = Specified Amount

5.2 OPTION B (Increasing Death Benefit) - Available by Request
    Death Benefit = Specified Amount + Account Value

5.3 You may change death benefit options subject to:
    (a) Evidence of insurability if increasing coverage
    (b) Payment of a $50 processing fee

5.4 Death benefit is paid minus any outstanding policy loans.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 6 - PARTIAL WITHDRAWALS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
6.1 You may make partial withdrawals from Account Value at any time.

6.2 Minimum withdrawal: $500
    Maximum: Account Value minus amount needed to keep policy in force

6.3 Withdrawal charges:
    Years 1-10: $25 per withdrawal
    Years 11+: No charge

6.4 Withdrawals reduce Account Value and may reduce death benefit 
    if Account Value falls below minimum required.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 7 - POLICY LOANS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
7.1 Maximum loan: 90% of current Account Value

7.2 Loan interest rate: Variable rate = Moody's Corporate Bond 
    Index + 1.5%, minimum 4%, adjusted annually each January 1

7.3 Interest may be paid annually or added to loan balance

7.4 Outstanding loan reduces death benefit dollar-for-dollar

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 8 - SURRENDER
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
8.1 Cash Surrender Value = Account Value - Surrender Charge - 
    Outstanding Loans

8.2 Surrender Charge Schedule (% of Specified Amount):
    Year 1: 8% | Year 5: 5% | Year 10: 2% | Year 15+: 0%

8.3 Upon surrender, the policy terminates and coverage ends.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 9 - LIMITATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
9.1 SUICIDE EXCLUSION: If insured dies by suicide within 24 months 
    of issue date, we pay only the Account Value, without interest.

9.2 CONTESTABILITY: We may contest this policy for material 
    misrepresentation within 2 years of issue date while insured 
    is living.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 10 - GRACE PERIOD AND LAPSE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
10.1 If Account Value is insufficient to pay monthly deductions, 
     we will notify you. Policy continues for 61 days.

10.2 If sufficient premium is not received within 61 days, policy 
     lapses.

10.3 Upon lapse, any Account Value in excess of surrender charges 
     will be paid to you automatically.
