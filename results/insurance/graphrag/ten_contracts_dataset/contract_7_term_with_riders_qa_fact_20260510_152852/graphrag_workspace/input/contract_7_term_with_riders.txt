POLICY DEFINITIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
"Disability" means the complete inability to perform the material 
and substantial duties of your regular occupation due to sickness 
or injury.

"Terminal Illness" means a condition expected to result in death 
within 24 months as certified by a physician.

"Chronic Illness" means the inability to perform at least 2 of 6 
Activities of Daily Living for at least 90 days.

SECTION 1: BASE POLICY COVERAGE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
1.1 We will pay the death benefit if the insured dies while this 
    policy is in force and all premiums have been paid.

1.2 Payment will be made to the beneficiary within 30 days of 
    receiving satisfactory proof of death.

1.3 EXCLUSION - SUICIDE: If death occurs by suicide within 2 years 
    of the issue date, we will pay only the premiums paid, without 
    interest.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 2: ATTACHED RIDERS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

RIDER 1: WAIVER OF PREMIUM DUE TO DISABILITY
Premium: $102/year

If you become totally disabled before age 60 and remain disabled 
for 6 consecutive months:
• All future premiums are waived
• Coverage continues in full force
• Waiver continues until you recover, return to work, or age 65
• Must submit annual proof of continuing disability

RIDER 2: ACCELERATED DEATH BENEFIT - TERMINAL ILLNESS
Premium: Included at no charge

If diagnosed with a terminal illness:
• May access up to 95% of death benefit while living
• Minimum advance: $10,000
• Maximum advance: $500,000
• Benefit reduced by actuarial discount (approximately 8% per year)
• Remaining death benefit paid to beneficiaries upon death

RIDER 3: ACCELERATED DEATH BENEFIT - CHRONIC ILLNESS
Premium: $48/year

If you become chronically ill and cannot perform 2+ Activities of 
Daily Living (bathing, dressing, eating, toileting, transferring, 
continence):
• May access up to 2% of face amount per month
• Maximum total advance: 50% of face amount
• No actuarial discount applied
• Must be certified by licensed healthcare practitioner

RIDER 4: CHILDREN'S TERM INSURANCE RIDER
Premium: $156/year

Provides $15,000 coverage on each eligible child:
• Covers all natural and legally adopted children
• Coverage from birth (or adoption) to age 25
• Automatically includes future children
• No medical exam required
• Children may convert to individual policy at age 25 without 
  evidence of insurability, up to 5 times the rider amount

Covered Children:
- Christopher Bennett Jr. (Age 8)
- Olivia Grace Bennett (Age 5)

RIDER 5: ACCIDENTAL DEATH BENEFIT
Premium: $94/year

Pays an additional $750,000 if death results from accidental injury:
• Accident must be the direct cause of death
• Death must occur within 90 days of accident
• Excludes: suicide, self-inflicted injury, war, aviation (as pilot/crew)
• No payment if insured was under influence of drugs/alcohol
• Terminates at insured's age 70

RIDER 6: GUARANTEED INSURABILITY OPTION
Premium: $89/year

Allows purchase of additional coverage without medical underwriting:
• May purchase up to $100,000 additional coverage per option date
• Option dates: Ages 40, 43, 46, 49 and at birth/adoption of child
• Must exercise within 90 days of option date
• Premium based on age and class at time of exercise
• All options terminate at age 50
