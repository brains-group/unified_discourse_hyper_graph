┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SECTION 1: IMPORTANT DEFINITIONS                                 │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
1.1 "Sub-Account" means a segregated investment account that 
    invests in shares of a designated mutual fund.

1.2 "Unit Value" means the value of one unit of a sub-account, 
    calculated daily based on fund performance.

1.3 "Accumulation Units" means units held in sub-accounts before 
    any distributions.

1.4 "Mortality and Expense Risk Charge" (M&E) is a daily charge 
    of 0.90% annually to cover insurance risks and administrative 
    costs.

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SECTION 2: INVESTMENT ALLOCATION                                 │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
2.1 Premium payments (after premium load) are allocated to 
    sub-accounts based on your investment instructions.

2.2 Available Sub-Accounts:
    • Equity Growth Fund
    • Balanced Allocation Fund
    • Government Bond Fund
    • International Equity Fund
    • Money Market Fund (guaranteed minimum 1.5%)
    • Real Estate Securities Fund

2.3 Current Allocation:
    Equity Growth Fund: 40%
    Balanced Allocation Fund: 30%
    Government Bond Fund: 20%
    Money Market Fund: 10%

2.4 You may change allocations at any time by written request 
    or through our online portal. No fee for first 12 changes 
    per year; $25 fee thereafter.

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SECTION 3: CHARGES AND FEES                                      │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
3.1 PREMIUM LOAD: 5.0% of each premium payment

3.2 MORTALITY & EXPENSE RISK CHARGE: 0.90% per annum of account 
    value, deducted daily

3.3 ADMINISTRATIVE CHARGE: $15 per month

3.4 COST OF INSURANCE: Monthly charge based on:
    (Face Amount at Risk) × (Monthly COI Rate) / 1,000
    Face Amount at Risk = Death Benefit - Account Value

3.5 FUND EXPENSES: Each sub-account has its own expense ratio 
    ranging from 0.45% to 1.25% annually (detailed in prospectus)

3.6 SURRENDER CHARGE SCHEDULE (% of premium paid):
    Year 1-2: 7% | Year 3-4: 5% | Year 5-6: 3% | Year 7+: 0%

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SECTION 4: DEATH BENEFIT OPTIONS                                 │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
4.1 OPTION I - Level Death Benefit
    Death Benefit = Face Amount
    Lower charges but no growth from investments

4.2 OPTION II - Variable Death Benefit (CURRENTLY SELECTED)
    Death Benefit = Face Amount + Account Value
    Higher charges but death benefit grows with account

4.3 You may switch between options subject to:
    • Proof of insurability for increases
    • Minimum face amount requirements
    • $50 administrative fee

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SECTION 5: INVESTMENT RISK DISCLOSURE                            │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
5.1 IMPORTANT: The account value and death benefit (under Option II) 
    are NOT guaranteed and will fluctuate based on sub-account 
    performance.

5.2 Investment risk is borne entirely by the policy owner.

5.3 Poor investment performance can cause the policy to lapse if 
    account value becomes insufficient to cover charges.

5.4 The Money Market sub-account has a guaranteed minimum rate of 
    1.5% but other sub-accounts have no guaranteed minimum return.

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SECTION 6: LOANS AND WITHDRAWALS                                 │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
6.1 POLICY LOANS:
    • Maximum: 90% of surrender value
    • Interest Rate: 5.5% per annum (fixed)
    • Loan amounts are moved to the Money Market sub-account
    • Reduces death benefit dollar-for-dollar

6.2 PARTIAL WITHDRAWALS:
    • Minimum: $1,000
    • Maximum: Account value minus $5,000
    • Processing fee: $50
    • Surrender charges may apply (see Section 3.6)
    • Reduces face amount proportionally

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SECTION 7: POLICY LAPSE AND REINSTATEMENT                        │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
7.1 If account value is insufficient to cover monthly deductions 
    for 61 consecutive days, the policy will lapse.

7.2 Lapse notice will be sent at least 30 days before lapse.

7.3 Reinstatement within 3 years requires:
    • Evidence of insurability
    • Payment of all back charges plus 6% interest
    • Repayment of outstanding loans

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  SECTION 8: EXCLUSIONS                                            │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
8.1 SUICIDE: If death occurs by suicide within 2 years of issue 
    or reinstatement, we pay only premiums paid minus withdrawals.

8.2 WAR EXCLUSION: Death while serving in military conflict in a 
    war zone is excluded if it occurs within 24 months of issue.

8.3 AVIATION: Death resulting from operating an aircraft as pilot 
    or crew member is excluded unless declared and approved with 
    extra premium.
