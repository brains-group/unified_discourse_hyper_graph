SECTION 4: DEFINITIONS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

4.1 "Death Benefit" means the amount payable to the beneficiary 
    upon the death of the insured during the policy term.

4.2 "Premium Due Date" means the 15th day of each month.

4.3 "Grace Period" means the 31-day period following the premium 
    due date during which coverage continues without lapse.

4.4 "Policy Anniversary" means the same day and month as the 
    issue date in each subsequent year.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 5: DEATH BENEFIT
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

5.1 We will pay the death benefit to the beneficiary if:
    (a) The insured dies while this policy is in force, and
    (b) We receive proof of death acceptable to us, and
    (c) The death occurs before the policy expiry date.

5.2 The death benefit will be paid in one lump sum unless the 
    beneficiary chooses an alternate settlement option.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 6: PREMIUM PAYMENTS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

6.1 Premiums are due monthly on the 15th of each month.

6.2 If a premium is not paid by the due date, a grace period of 
    31 days is allowed. Coverage continues during the grace period.

6.3 If the premium is not paid by the end of the grace period, 
    the policy will lapse and coverage will terminate.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 7: EXCLUSIONS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

7.1 SUICIDE CLAUSE: If the insured commits suicide within two 
    years from the issue date, our liability is limited to a 
    refund of premiums paid.

7.2 No other exclusions apply to this policy.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 8: CONVERSION PRIVILEGE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

8.1 You may convert this policy to a permanent life insurance 
    policy without evidence of insurability.

8.2 Conversion must occur before the earlier of:
    (a) The policy expiry date, or
    (b) The insured's 65th birthday.

8.3 The premium for the new policy will be based on:
    (a) The insured's attained age at conversion
    (b) The amount of coverage converted
    (c) The class of permanent policy selected

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 9: CLAIMS PROCEDURE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

9.1 To claim the death benefit, the beneficiary must submit:
    (a) Completed claim form
    (b) Certified copy of the death certificate
    (c) Original policy document (if available)

9.2 Claims should be sent to:
    SecureLife Insurance Company
    Claims Department
    P.O. Box 5000, Hartford, CT 06102

9.3 We will pay valid claims within 30 days of receiving all 
    required documentation.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 10: GENERAL PROVISIONS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

10.1 INCONTESTABILITY: We cannot contest this policy after it 
     has been in force during the insured's lifetime for two 
     years from the issue date.

10.2 MISSTATEMENT OF AGE OR SEX: If the age or sex of the 
     insured has been misstated, we will adjust the death 
     benefit to what the premiums paid would have purchased 
     at the correct age and sex.

10.3 ASSIGNMENT: You may assign this policy. We are not 
     responsible for the validity of any assignment. An 
     assignment is not binding on us until we receive written 
     notice at our home office.
